A love letter to limited editions

Long before we had words, we had images.

We pressed our hands to cave walls.

And ever since, we have filled the places we live with things that matter.

A wall is only a wall.

Hang one true thing on it, and the room changes.

It stops being somewhere you pass through.

And becomes somewhere you are.
