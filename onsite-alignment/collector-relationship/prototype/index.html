Four experiences, one collector relationship.

A dormant buyer becomes a collector through the concierge desk, settles into membership as their home, and the deepest collectors reach a commission. Beneath every purchase sits the returns-confidence layer that makes each step feel safe to take.

Built on verified online-B2C collector data (LUMAS DWH, 0 B2B lines). Names are placeholders; behaviour, collections and artworks are real.

Each prototype reconstructs a real collector from line-level data (available from 2022); the people featured joined from 2022, so their collections are complete. Artwork images are live from the LUMAS catalogue. The commission proposal and edition framing are illustrative; the holdings behind them are real.
