What could go wrong

What we're watching.

Artist contracts

Commission and membership first-look depend on rights and resale terms. Verify with 2 to 3 top artists before building.

Cannibalization

Membership credit must drive net-new spend, not discount existing sales. Pilot on high-value collectors where the math is cleanest.

A modest online base, not headline "whales"

The biggest accounts skew B2B and in-gallery; genuine online private collectors are smaller. The sizing already reflects this.

Execution capacity

Concierge and commission are deliberately human-led and low-volume. Scale comes from membership, not headcount.
